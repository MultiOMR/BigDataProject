ttk::treeview::between   tv item1 item2  

between $tv $item1 $item2 – Returns a list of all items between $item1 and $item2, in preorder traversal order. $item1 and $item2 may be in either order.

NOTES: This routine is O(N) in the size of the tree. There's probably a way to do this that's O(N) in the number of items returned, but I'm not clever enough to figure it out.
